Pure Basic primarily collaborates with brands that are GOTS certified, which is one of the highest standards for organic fibers. In this way, they can vouch 100% for the sustainability of their products.

By buying clothing made of organic cotton at Pure Basic, you make a responsible choice for you and your surroundings. You help ensure environmentally and socially responsible production and look after our land.

Pure Basic takes great care that their products are sustainable. Their environmental, health and organic certification labels are proof of that.

The certificates make it easier for you as a consumer to see if the product you are interested in meets proper standards and whether it has been produced under proper conditions.

GOTS-certified clothing is undoubtedly a step in the right direction for the environment, the workers and yourself.
