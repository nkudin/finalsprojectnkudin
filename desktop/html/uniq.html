Every item of clothing you purchase from UNIQ reduces the amount of waste, energy and water consumption, as well as your carbon footprint and the amount of harmful chemicals involved in the production of textiles and clothes.

UNIQ chooses second chances over low-cost mass production of clothes.Every item of clothing is more friendly to your body, every piece is unique and not a cheap replica of an iconic model or a trend from the past.

Buying a secondhand product makes you an active part of the sustainable development that supports our lifestyle and in turn the planet we live on.

UNIQ provides sustainable alternatives and products to for an individual style and helps in maintaining quality and up to date clothing and a selection of vintage items.
